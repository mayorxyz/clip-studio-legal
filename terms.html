Terms of Service — Clip_Studio

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Clip_Studio is a personal tool that uploads pre-edited video clips to the operator's own TikTok account as private drafts, using TikTok's Content Posting API.

1. Use of Service

This app is used solely by its operator to manage their own TikTok content. It does not offer services to third parties.

2. Account Access

The app connects to TikTok via official OAuth login. The operator authorizes the app to upload draft video content to their own account only.

3. Content

All content uploaded is created or edited by the operator prior to upload. No content is published automatically — drafts require manual review and posting within the TikTok app.

4. Termination

The operator may revoke app access at any time via TikTok account settings.
