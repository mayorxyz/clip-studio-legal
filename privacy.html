Privacy Policy — Clip_Studio

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Clip_Studio is a personal-use tool and does not collect, store, or share data from any third party.

1. Data Collected

The app uses TikTok's OAuth login to obtain an access token tied to the operator's own TikTok account. This token is stored locally and used only to upload video drafts via the Content Posting API.

2. Data Use

No user data is collected, sold, or shared with any third party. Video files processed by the app remain on the operator's local device until uploaded directly to TikTok.

3. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by the operator. No data is retained on external servers.

4. Third-Party Sharing

None. This app does not share any data with third parties.
